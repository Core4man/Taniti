Four Star hotel

This option is the most popular and gives many guests the satisfaction of staying in a resort Located in central Taniti, it has a nice private beach, discounts to local attractions, and a shuttle from the airport or port. Highly recommended for weddings, honeymoons, and business trips and for all group sizes.

Hotel

This option helps support local families and lets you have more options on the location you want to stay at. This is also a cheaper option and is more for vacation goers and groups of 4 or smaller. You would have to have a rental car, rent a bike, or take the local buses to arrive from the airport or dock.

Hostel/Bed and Breakfast

This option also supports local families and allows for a more adventurous stay on the island. You will meet many new people; local and foreign. It is a very economic way to stay in Taniti and recommended for vacation goers and groups of 4 or smaller. Like a hotel, you will need to rent a car or bike, or take the local buses to arrive.
