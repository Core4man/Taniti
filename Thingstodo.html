Local Attractions:

  • Beaches: Many people come to Taniti to come and see the many beaches on the island for sunbathing. You can also go snorkeling and swimming near these beaches.
  • Rainforest: The lush rainforest that covers parts of Taniti attracts many people to the beautiful greenery and exotic wildlife that resides there. Also inside the rainforest is a zipline that allows tourists to see the rainforest as you glide in the sky above the trees.
  • Volcano: This fiery mountain is the tallest point of the island and can be seen either by hiking up to the rim of the volcano or take a helicopter ride over it.
  • Local history Museum: Nothing more local than the local history museum. You can learn about the natives of the island and how the island has gone through each part of history with many artifacts, live performances, and storytelling from local elders.
  • Boat and bus tours: These activities are very popular and allow tourists to see and experience the island with the help of local experts.
  • Taniti City: This is the largest city in Taniti and where most tourists stay and explore. The airport, the bay, native architecture, and many businesses and restaurants are located here as well as local bus transportation.
